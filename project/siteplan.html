Site Plan: Industrial Conveyor Belts

Project Planning Document

Site Name

Industrial Conveyor Solutions

This name shows the focus of the site that is to provide detailed information and resources about industrial conveyor belts for businesses and professionals.

Domain: industrialconveyorsolutions.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform businesses and engineers about the different types of industrial conveyor belts, their uses, benefits, and maintenance requirements. It will also provide contact information for product inquiries.

Scenarios

  • Which type of conveyor belt is best for transporting heavy materials?
  • How can I request a maintenance service for my company's conveyor system?

Color Scheme

Primary: Dark Blue (used for headings, navigation)
Secondary: Yellow (used for accents, highlights)
Tertiary: Teal (#009688) (used for buttons and links)

Typography

Headings: DM Serif Text

Body Text: PT Sans
